// How It Works

Eight Steps to Every Argument

The same process a well-funded defense team would use — available free to every family.

01
Read Everything First
Every document you upload is fully read before the AI responds. Police reports, plea transcripts, PCR orders, attorney letters — everything is analyzed for legally significant details, dates, contradictions.
02
Build the Timeline
A complete chronological timeline is constructed from earliest event to today. Every date matters — filing deadlines, statute of limitations, AEDPA clock — all calculated from your facts.
03
Catalog Attorney Failures
Every attorney who touched the case is identified. Every failure — failure to investigate, failure to file, failure to advise, conflict of interest — is cataloged for the Strickland analysis.
04
Find Unused Evidence
Witnesses never called. Evidence never requested. Expert testimony never retained. Police reports never obtained. Every piece of available-but-unused evidence is identified.
05
Map Facts to Law
Every fact is matched to every applicable constitutional doctrine — IAC, Brady, Napue, Boykin, Martinez, actual innocence, Batson, Ramos, Ake, Dusky, and more.
06
Draft the Arguments
Complete filing language is generated for every viable claim — the strongest possible version, with real case citations, factual allegations, and legal standards applied to your specific facts.
07
Build the Rebuttals
Every State counterargument is anticipated and addressed inside the filing. Procedural default, AEDPA deference, strategic decision — all rebutted before the State even raises them.
08
Action Plan with Deadlines
Plain-language instructions: what to file, where to file it, when the deadline is, what address to use, what order to file in. Every step laid out so nothing is missed.
